Phase 3 (Years 7-8) Unit Collections

Professionally curated, knowledge-rich units for your Years 7-8 classroom.

Year 7 Units

Term 1

Te Tiriti and Early Colonial Years

Te Tiriti, He Whakaputanga, early governors, New Zealand Wars, source work.

 Term 2

Rivers, Glaciation and Local Landscapes

River processes, glacial landforms, map/aerial-photo skills, field data.

 Term 3

Democracy, Citizenship and Civic Participation

NZ representative democracy, MMP, Māori electorates, citizen actions, simulated election.

 Term 4

Industrial Revolution and Personal Finance

Causes/consequences of Industrial Revolution; budgeting, trade-offs, currency comparison.

Year 8 Units

Term 1

Liberal Era and Women’s Suffrage

Liberal reforms, women’s suffrage, social policy, primary source analysis.

 Term 2

European Imperial Expansion and Case Study

Causes/impacts of imperialism; British expansion in India case study; historiography.

 Term 3

Population Change and Hazard Management

Demographic Transition Model, population pyramids, migration; tropical storms, earthquakes, volcanoes; case studies and policy recommendations.

 Term 4

Personal Finance, Government Budget and Constitutional Models

Credit/debt/interest, consumer rights, inflation; analysing a government budget; NZ’s uncodified constitution comparison.

Our Mission

Tāhuhu Kōrero is dedicated to supporting kaiako (teachers) within Kāhui Ako ki Ōtākaro in bringing the Aotearoa New Zealand Social Sciences curriculum to life. We aim to provide high-quality, accessible, and engaging resources that honour Te Tiriti o Waitangi and reflect the diverse histories and voices of our nation.
