Curriculum Framework

Understand, Know, and Do

The new curriculum is structured around three key elements to help ākonga make sense of the past, present, and future.

Understand

The "big ideas" and contexts that are central to Aotearoa New Zealand's histories and social landscape.

Know

The specific people, places, events, and stories that illustrate the "Understand" contexts.

Do

The skills ākonga use to think critically, examine sources, and explore different perspectives.

Our Mission

Tāhuhu Kōrero is dedicated to supporting kaiako (teachers) in bringing the Aotearoa New Zealand Social Sciences curriculum to life. We aim to provide high-quality, accessible, and engaging resources that honour Te Tiriti o Waitangi and reflect the diverse histories and voices of our nation.
